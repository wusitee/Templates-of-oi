> choose <mark>one</mark> from the `[]`, `[abc]` means that either a or b or c can be used.

> not, `^abc` means that a or b or c can be chosen.

> choose <mark>one group of letters</mark> in it, `(s|es|er)` means that `s`
or `es` or `er` can be
> chosen.
